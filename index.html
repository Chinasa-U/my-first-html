The Whispering Wind

In the quiet of the night,

When the stars begin to gleam,

The wind carries a tale,

Soft as a fleeting dream.

It rustles through the trees,

A melody so sweet,

Whispers of the ancient earth,

Where sky and forest meet.

Listen closely, hear it sing,

Of love and loss, of time and spring,

A story woven in the night,

By the moon's gentle light.
